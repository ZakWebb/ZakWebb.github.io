Education and Research Interests

I am a PhD candidate at the Institute for Quantum Computing at the University of Waterloo. I'm studying under Andrew Childs, and will be getting my PhD in the Physics & Astronomy Department, with a focus in Quantum Information. I received my undergraduate degree in Physics, Mathematics, and Computer Science from the University of Washington in 2011.

I am interested in the computational power of various quantum systems, as well as various topics in quantum information. I am particularly interested in Hamiltonian complexity, quantum walk, and quantum derandomization.
